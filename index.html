About This Project

Storing, retrieving, visualizing, and communicating data.

Project Concept

With a list of 100 major US cities, look at what cities talk about each other (including themselves) and whether it is positive or negative. We could also look at whether the sentiment changes based on day of week, time of day, weather, sports, or major events.

Project Goals

To show an overview of interesting findings from our exploration of the data and to allow the user to explore the data in detail to their own liking by filtering by city (or state or region perhaps), type of weather, positive or negative sentiment, etc.



Data Source

Twitter streaming API. Sample of tweets from July 7, 2014 through August ?, 2014.

Storage & Retrieval

Amazon Web Services. Apache Solr.

Visualization Tools

Tableau. d3.js.

Known Limitations

We don't have access to all tweets in a given time period, only a limited sample. All tweets don't have a geolocation, nor do all users have a location designated. Limited to the top 100 US cities. Sentiment analysis isn't perfect.
